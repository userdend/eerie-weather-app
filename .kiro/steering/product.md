# Product Overview

## Eerie Weather App

An immersive 3D weather visualization experience that combines real-time weather data with atmospheric visuals and sound. The app features a spooky floating house that reacts to weather conditions from cities worldwide.

## Core Features

- **7 Weather Effects**: Clear, Cloudy, Rain, Thunderstorm, Snow, Fog, and Windy
- **Real-time Weather Data**: Search any city to visualize current weather conditions
- **Manual Control**: Override API data with manual weather selection and intensity adjustment
- **Dynamic 3D Scene**: Floating house model with particle systems for each weather type
- **Lightning System**: Jagged bolts with branching effects and dramatic flashes for thunderstorms
- **Ambient Audio**: Looping weather sounds that match the visual effects
- **Favorites System**: Save frequently checked cities for quick access
- **Interactive Camera**: OrbitControls for exploring the 3D scene

## Design Theme

Spooky, eerie atmosphere with dark color palette (deep blue-purple backgrounds), custom "October Crow" font, and blood-red accents. The app maintains a haunted aesthetic even in clear weather conditions.
